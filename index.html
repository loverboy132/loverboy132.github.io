Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to use?

Yes! Basic use is free. Some advanced features require a subscription. You can showcase your work, apply for jobs, and earn through referrals at no cost.

How do I earn money?

Through referrals (₦100 per successful referral), jobs as an apprentice, and future competitions. Members can also earn by posting jobs and connecting with talented apprentices.

What's the difference between Members and Apprentices?

Members are clients who post jobs and hire apprentices. Apprentices are creative professionals who apply for jobs and showcase their work. You can be both!

How do payments work?

All payments are processed securely through Paystack. Members pay upfront for jobs, and apprentices receive payment upon job completion and approval.

Is my work protected?

Yes! You retain full ownership of your creative work. We only use your content to showcase it on our platform and help you get discovered.

How do referrals work?

Share your unique referral link with friends. When they sign up and complete their first job or post their first job, you both earn ₦100!
